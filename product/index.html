Social Network Monitoring

Financial professionals give significant attention to the many economic reports released each month. However, the data available in these reports significantly lags consumer habits by up to several months. SocialTradr bridges that gap by providing real-time social media analytics to financial professionals. By measuring online public opinion, SocialTradr offers insight about how companies and their brands are being perceived online that investors can use to make informed decisions before the release of the next economic report.
