Social Network Monitoring

People are constantly talking and sharing their opinions about the products and services they use. SocialTradr collects these opinions from Facebook, Twitter, and several other social networks and analyses all of it to bring it to you in a clear, concise manner. Understanding what people are saying and how their opinions change over time can help you make a more informed investment decision.
