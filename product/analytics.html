Intelligent Analytics

SocialTradr provides the analytics you need to improve your investment decisions. Each message is given a sentiment score based on our proprietary algorithm. Changes in sentiment over time can provide investment ideas and solidify your theses. Follow overall brand sentiment and volume, or delve deeper and find out what people are saying about specific keywords. SocialTradr provides the data and tools you need for the companies and brands you follow.
